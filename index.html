Schizzo

Schizzo ($SCHIZZ) is a community-driven meme token built on the Ethereum blockchain. No presales. Currently, the majority of the supply is held by the owner, with plans to distribute it widely to the community, ensuring fair access and broad decentralization over time. A small portion of the supply is reserved for the team to support development, marketing, and ecosystem growth. Built for fun, powered by the community, and open to anyone who wants to join the movement.
